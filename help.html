Help Center

Frequently asked questions about your commute.

How is the fare calculated?

Fares are based on the total distance traveled between stations. We follow the standard MRT Line 3 fare matrix with a base fare of ₱50.00.

Can I get a student discount?

Yes! Use our Fare Matrix tool and check the "Discount" toggle to see your adjusted price (20% off for Seniors, Students, and PWDs).

What are the operating hours?

eCABI follows the MRT 3 schedule: First train at 4:30 AM and Last train at 10:30 PM daily.
